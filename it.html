IT Scene

The IT scene in the San Francisco Bay area is like no other. Comprised of tech companies of all sizes and calibers, the bay area is the place to be if you want to be in tech. Even more, the iconic Silicon Valley is a mere 45 minutes away! Silicon Valley is known as the U.S. center for innovative tech companies and is home to some of the largest tech companies in the world!

Top Places to Work

Apple

Apple became the world’s first public company to be valued at $1 trillion dollars. There are people all over the world who use Apple products and services every minute of the day. Mac, iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch - all products loved by Apple fans and made by Apple employees. As an Apple employee, you’ll be working for arguably the most popular tech company and working on arguably the most popular products. If you are an Apple fan, working here is a no brainer!

Facebook

Are you into keeping people connected across the world? If the answer is yes, Facebook may be the company for you. Facebook estimated that 2.2 billion people use Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram, or Messenger each day. Employees have the pleasure of working on these services to make sure all those users are happy. If you are a Facebook or Instagram lover, you will love working here!

Google

Google is one of the most well-known technology companies in the world. Employees have the opportunity to work on products, apps, and services that people use worldwide. YouTube, AndroidOS, Chrome, and G Suite are just a few products/services Google employees get to work on. If you are a fan of google products/services, look no further! (Fun fact: employees are allowed to bring their pets to work!).

Amazon

People around the nation use Amazon products/services 24/7. Employees have the opportunity to work in a variety of different areas such as e-commerce, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence. Amazon started off as an online bookseller, and now has become one of the world’s most valuable companies! If you are an Amazon fan, this is your stop!

Twitter

Twitter is a popular social media platform that millions of people use each day. The platform is so popular that even the President of The United States uses it daily! As an employee, you will enjoy the fun, collaborative environment. Twitter is known for providing a workplace culture that is much different than any traditional workplace. If you love Twitter and tweet daily, this is the place for you!
