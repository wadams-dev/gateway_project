Popular Places to Visit

Golden Gate Bridge

Price: Free to the public.

Target Age Group: All ages.

Description: The Golden Gate Bridge is a iconic suspension bridge that spans 1.7 miles to connect the city of San Francisco to Marin County, California. Thousands of people commute across this bridge every day; taking in its beauty and historical significance. At the time of its opening in 1937, it was the longest and the tallest suspension bridge in the world. To learn more about the Golden Gate Bridge, click here.

Golden Gate Park

Price: There is no charge to enter the park itself, however there are attractions throughout the park that may cost.

Target Age Group: All ages.

Description: One of California’s largest urban parks, Golden Gate Park is over 1,000 acres with intricate amenities that include several gardens and museums. Millions of people visit annually for a range of activities from picnics to weddings. Golden Gate Park is the third most visited park in the US, following behind Central Park and Lincoln Memorial. Popular attractions throughout the park include the Japanese Tea Garden, Conservatory of Flowers, and the California Academy of Sciences. To learn more about the park, click here.

Alcatraz Island

Price: 5 different tours are offered. Pricing depends on age of attendee and tour selection. Tickets are generally $24.40 for a child and $39.90 for an adult. Click here for in-depth tour pricing.

Target Age Group: Ages 5 and up. Behind The Scenes Tour only admits ages 13 and up.

Description: Often referred to as "The Rock", Alcatraz Island once served as a lighthouse, a military fort, and a prison. It was home to some of the most notorious prisoners of the time such as Al Capone and Machine Gun Kelly. Being surrounded by water, Alcatraz was believed to be inescapable. Now the island serves as a famous tourist attraction and landmark. To learn more about Alcatraz, click here.

Pier 39

Price: Free to the public, however there are a number of restuarants and attractions on the pier that cost.

Target Age Group: All ages.

Description: Pier 39 is a major tourist attraction that is composed of restuarants, shops, and attractions such as sea lions, bay cruises, and Aquarium of the Bay. To learn more about the pier click here.

Union Square

Price: Free to the public.

Target Age Group: All ages.

Description: Union Square is known for being a popular shopping plaza in downtown San Fransisco. The district includes several shopping centers, restaurants, theaters, and hotels in the surrounding blocks. To learn more about Union Square, click here.
