San Francisco, California

Welcome to San Francisco

San Francisco is the cultural, commercial, and financial center of Northern California. Also referred to as the “Bay Area” or “San Fran”, San Francisco is known for its cool summers, fog, steep hills, diverse architecture, and landmarks. A few popular landmarks include the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, The painted ladies, and Lombard Street.
